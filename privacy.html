Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Eat My Bubbles is a personal swim tracking app designed for simplicity and clarity. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Information We Collect

The app stores swim session data that you manually enter, such as distance, time, and workout details. This data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced to your personal Firebase account.

No personal information such as your name, email, or location is collected by the app.

How Your Data Is Used

Your swim data is used solely to display your workout history and statistics within the app. It is not shared with any third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to sync with Firebase, your data is stored securely in your own Firebase account under your control.

Third‑Party Services

The app may integrate with third‑party services such as Samsung Health. Any data accessed from these services is used only within the app and is not transmitted elsewhere.

Your Choices

You may delete your swim data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.
